AI / ML Speech Deep Learning
A neural approach to multi-speaker voice cloning in english using fastspeech2 with ecapa-tdnn speaker representations
A few-shot multi-speaker voice cloning system for the English language using FastSpeech 2 modeling and ECAPA-TDNN speaker embeddings — enabling high-quality speaker-specific speech synthesis.
  • Few-shot voice cloning with just a few minutes of reference audio
  • Multi-speaker support using ECAPA-TDNN speaker embeddings
  • FastSpeech 2 non-autoregressive TTS for fast, parallel synthesis
  • HiFi-GAN vocoder for high-fidelity waveform reconstruction
  • Trained on SLR60(LibriTTS train-clean-100 and train-clean-360 subsets)
  • Audio preprocessing: denoising, normalization, mel-spectrogram extraction
